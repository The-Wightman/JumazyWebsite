ABOUT JUMAZI

Jumanji and labyrinth

Labyrinth is a popular maze based board game known and loved by many people and Jumanji is a board game from a popular movie which many people are familiar with. The idea of Jumazi is to combine aspects of the mentioned board games to create a unique game which can provide endless hours of entertainment.


Pseudo Maze generation

Every game of Jumazi is never the same. With our randomly generated maze, every game a new maze is created. This will keep each game fresh and you will never know who wins.


Multiplayer

Jumazi is a game that supports up to 4 players, So it can be enjoyed with all friends and family.
